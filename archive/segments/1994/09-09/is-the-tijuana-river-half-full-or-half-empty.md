---
title: Is the Tijuana River Half Full, or Half Empty?
slug: is-the-tijuana-river-half-full-or-half-empty
date: '1994-09-09'
category: Segments
summary: San Diego County, California has experienced beach closings due to raw sewage outflow from Mexico. Reporter Bebe Crouse visits a prototype sewage plant which treats sewage as a resource rather than a problem. It's being offered as an alternative to a massive treatment plant, towards which the US Government is contributing half a billion dollars.
