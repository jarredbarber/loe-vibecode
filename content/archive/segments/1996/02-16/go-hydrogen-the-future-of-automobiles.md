title: 'Go Hydrogen?: The Future of Automobiles'
slug: go-hydrogen-the-future-of-automobiles
date: '1996-02-16'
category: Segments
summary: In 1896, two engineers in Stuttgart, Germany invented the internal combustion car engine. Now, reporter Chris Brooks and producer Sandy Tolan return to Stuttgart to examine whether the world has made a cheap and dirty “deal with the devil", in obtaining this quick and easy transportation mode. The solution of hydrogen fueling is explored as a clean, though less glamorous, option for the future.
