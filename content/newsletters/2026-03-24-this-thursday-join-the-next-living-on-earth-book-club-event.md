# Join the next Living on Earth Book Club event - featuring Hannah Ritchie!

![](https://mcusercontent.com/9f9ebdcfa232a532a7e70746b/images/29cdc908-3b07-ba72-6e86-cb887c26eb87.jpg)

## Hannah will join us to talk about her new book [*Clearing the Air: A Hopeful Guide to Solving Climate Change in 50 Questions and 50 Answers*](https://bookshop.org/p/books/clearing-the-air-a-hopeful-guide-to-solving-climate-change-in-50-questions-and-answers-hannah-ritchie/3ab3b4f9a34ee2e1?ean=9780262052740\&next=t\&next=t\&affiliate=20556). 

### "If you want a book that explains the climate challenge without doom or denial, Clearing the Air is a must-read. It’s a hopeful reminder that while the problem is enormous, the progress is real." Gates Notes 

## Thursday, March 265:00 PM EDTOnline & free! - [REGISTER HERE](https://umassboston.zoom.us/webinar/register/9717737376037/WN_IDPJLtfhS6q7YNV3nGJD4g?mc_cid=3f77c2be82\&mc_eid=UNIQID#/registration)

![](https://mcusercontent.com/9f9ebdcfa232a532a7e70746b/images/4994a3c6-3d41-1a16-c6a4-2b9ea6c6ef66.jpg)

We can’t afford to delay climate action, but with all the shouting and disagreement, it’s hard to know where to turn.

The first piece of good news is that Ritchie is here with answers and the steps we need to take right now. Using simple, clear data, she tackles questions such as: Is it too late? Won’t we run out of minerals? Aren’t we too polarized? The second piece of good news: The truth is far more hopeful than you might think.

The Living on Earth Book Club, the [UMass Boston School for the Environment](https://www.umb.edu/environment/), and our media partner [Inside Climate News](https://insideclimatenews.org/) proudly present this free, live conversation between Hannah Ritchie and Living on Earth Executive Producer Steve Curwood. Looking for a copy of the book? Consider purchasing through Living on Earth + [Bookshop](https://bookshop.org/a/20556/9780262052740), which supports indie bookstores and nonprofits like LOE.
