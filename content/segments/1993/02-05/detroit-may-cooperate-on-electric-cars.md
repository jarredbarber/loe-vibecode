title: Detroit May Cooperate on Electric Cars
slug: detroit-may-cooperate-on-electric-cars
date: '1993-02-05'
category: Segments
summary: Paul Eisenstein reports from Detroit on the US auto industry's efforts to meet the 1998 deadline for mass-producing electric cars. The EPA has given the go-ahead for California and other states to mandate the sale of electric cars, and concerns over lagging technology may lead to increased cooperation between the big three.
