---
title: Fort Ord - Military Base or Wildlife Refuge?
slug: fort-ord-military-base-or-wildlife-refuge
date: '1994-03-25'
category: Segments
summary: Claire Greene reports from central California on the closing of an unlikely refuge for rare and endangered species — Fort Ord Military Base. Set aside at the turn of the century for combat training, a large part of Fort Ord's sixteen thousand acres has become a time capsule of unspoiled land, harboring plants and animals that have disappeared from neighboring areas under pressure from development.
